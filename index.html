FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Before we get started.

What areas do you service?

We serve the GTA, Caledon, Bolton, Tottenham and surrounding areas, with broader Southern Ontario coverage for the right projects.

Do you work directly with builders and general contractors?

Yes. Custom residential and commercial contractor work is a major part of our business. We coordinate with project managers, site supervisors and other trades.

Can you handle permits and ESA inspections?

Yes. Where required, we coordinate ESA notification and inspection as part of the electrical scope.

Do you install EV chargers?

Yes. We install home, workplace and commercial EV charging after confirming service capacity, wiring route and equipment requirements.

What is your workmanship warranty?

We provide a one-year workmanship warranty from project completion.
